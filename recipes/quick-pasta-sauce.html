Quick Pasta Sauce

Pasta Sauce is made by combining a variety of ingredients such as tomatoes, garlic, onions, olive oil, herbs, and spices, and simmering them together to create a thick and flavorful sauce. Depending on the recipe, other ingredients such as vegetables, meat, or cheese may also be added to enhance the flavor and texture of the sauce. Pasta sauce can be served with a variety of pasta shapes, including spaghetti, penne, or fettuccine, and can be a quick and easy meal option that is also versatile and customizable.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over low heat; add garlic and saut√© until tender, about 2 minutes.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer, stirring occasionally, until slightly thickened, 15 to 20 minutes.
  3. Serve it !
