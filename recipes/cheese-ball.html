Cheese Ball

A fried cheese ball is a savory appetizer made by mixing together cheese, breadcrumbs, herbs, and other seasonings, forming them into small balls, and then deep frying them until crispy and golden brown on the outside, and gooey and melt on the inside. The cheese used can vary depending on personal preference, but popular choices include mozzarella, cheddar, or a mixture of different cheeses. Once fried, the cheese balls are often served hot and crispy with a dipping sauce, such as marinara, ranch, or aioli, to add extra flavor and creaminess. The result is a delicious and satisfying snack that is perfect for sharing with friends or family.

Ingredients

  • 300g of mozzarella cheese
  • 300g of parmesan cheese
  • ½ teaspoon of black pepper
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 spoon of cornstarch
  • 1 spoon of dried oregano
  • 100g of Fideo noodles
  • oil to fry
  • salt to taste

Directions

  1. Grate the cheeses as finely as possible
  2. Put ½ teaspoon of black pepper
  3. Put 1 spoon of cornstarch
  4. Put 1 egg
  5. Put 1 spoon of dried oregano
  6. Mix well and put in the fridge
  7. When the mixture is cold, bread the balls using beaten egg and broken Fideo noodles
  8. Fry the balls in an oil at a temperature of 180ºC
  9. Serve it !
